About Us

With the rise of non-crypto Digital Banks, NeoBanks, and that of Cryptocurrency Banks. It was only but a matter of time before a hybrid of all three were to take place. Bank of Kingston (BOK) strives to empower the under banked, & the unbanked.

BOK is a CeDeFi product in the Digital Banking space that is a hybrid of high yield CeDeFi products and services. BOK will apply for an offshore Digital Banking license, while partnering with a tier 1 BaaS and SaaS provider to be jurisdictional compliant.
