About Us

TrueSPAC Gold is but one of three mining related tokens, out of six TrueSPAC tokens. TrueSPAC Diamond (tDMD), TrueSPAC Mining & Mineral (tMNG) are the other two mining related tokens. The three non mining tokens are TrueSPAC Investment (iSPAC), TrueSPAC Smart Contracts (TSPAC), TrueSPAC Payments (SPACp).

Tawfiki Finance LLC, signed an agreement with Soledad Mining Inc., in respect to Gold mining operations in Guyana, which would see TrueSPAC Investment (iSPAC) Securities token conduct an Securities Token Offering (STO), for expansion of existing Gold mining operations in Guyana.
